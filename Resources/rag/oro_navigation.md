# OroCommerce Admin Navigation

## Customer Management
- Customer Users: /admin/customer/user/ (list), /admin/customer/user/view/{id} (detail)
- Customers: /admin/customer/ (list), /admin/customer/view/{id} (detail)
- Customer Groups: /admin/customer/group/

## Sales
- Orders: /admin/order/ (list), /admin/order/view/{id} (detail)
- Shopping Lists: /admin/shopping-list/ (list)
- Quotes: /admin/sale/quote/ (list)
- RFQs: /admin/rfp/request/ (list)

## Product Management
- Products: /admin/product/ (list), /admin/product/view/{id} (detail)
- Categories: /admin/catalog/category/ (manage category tree)
- Price Lists: /admin/pricing/price-list/ (list)
- Inventory: /admin/inventory/level/ (manage inventory levels)

## Marketing
- Promotions: /admin/promotion/ (list)
- Landing Pages: /admin/cms/page/ (list)
- Content Blocks: /admin/cms/content-block/ (list)
- Web Catalogs: /admin/web-catalog/ (list)

## System Configuration
- General Setup: /admin/config/system/general-setup
- Integrations: /admin/config/system/integrations
- User Management: /admin/user/ (list users)
- Roles: /admin/user/role/ (list roles)

## Shipping & Tax
- Shipping Rules: /admin/shipping-rule/ (list)
- Tax Rules: /admin/tax/rule/ (list)
- Payment Terms: /admin/payment-term/ (list)

## Reports
- Reports: /admin/report/
- Dashboards: /admin/dashboard/
